WAM Roiter Hot Water Steam Hose

A mandrel-built delivery hose specifically engineered for washing and cleaning purposes using hot or cold water. Widely used as washdown hose in dairies, creameries, milk, wine, and general food industries. Also used as delivery hose for hot water mixed with detergents. Used for sterilization in CIP food industry workshops.

  • Standard: FDA Title 21 Art. 177.2600 for aqueous foods. Phthalate-free. High resistance to many aggressive cleaning agents.
  • Temperature range: −30°c – 100°c
  • Safety factor: 3:1
  • Tube material: smooth white synthetic rubber
  • Cover material: synthetic blue rubber, wrapped or smooth fabric impression
  • Marking: continuous transfer tape: ROITER AC220 WASHDOWN - HOT WATER - WP 20BAR / 300PSI - MADE IN ITALY
