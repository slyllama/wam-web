Job Vacancies

Pipeline Industries Limited is a progressive and innovative manufacturing and supply company serving a wide range of industries, from heavy industrial, infrastructural, marine, automotive, food, and rural, right through to the home handyman. We combine customer service with hands-on servicing, product selection and assembly of products to match customer requirements.

Experienced Person (Customer Services)

This job offers great potential and opportunity.

If you are a practical person with basic engineering/mechanical, welding, hose assembly, testing and stock control ability, who has confidence to serve the needs of customers with our range of products and services, including hose assembly, then process their order, and have a desire to cement a good long-term future, or a more senior person who can pass knowledge on to our young future generation, you should apply to one of our branches near you.

Note, Our methods techniques and procedures will be provided in training.

Send application with CV to johnny@hoseshop.co.nz or drop off to the manager of the branch closets to where you live.

Young Person (Customer Services)

Alongside our experienced people, we like to have keen and diligent young people around 18 to 30 (about) who can work with us, learn and gain skills. If the person is willing to put in the effort, young persons can build themselves a good future and learn computer, inventory control, product knowledge, basic engineering, welding, hose assembly and testing.

Send application with CV to johnny@hoseshop.co.nz or drop off to the manager of the branch closets to where you live.
